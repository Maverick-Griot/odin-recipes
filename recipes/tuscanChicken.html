Tuscan Chicken

Description

This simple recipe features chicken in a delicious creamy sauce with spinach and sun-dried tomatoes. Best part? You can prepare and cook this in under an hour. Pairs wonderfully with cheese crusted french bread.

  • Prep Time: 5 Minutes
  • Cook Time: 40 Minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 Boneless Chicken Breast
  • 3 Cups of Spinach
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Cherry Tomatoes - Halved
  • 1/2 Cups of Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 Cup of freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1 Teaspoon of dried Oregano
  • 3 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 3 Garlic Cloves - Minced
  • Kosher Salt

Process

  1. Add oil and chicken to a skillet over medium heat. Season with salt, pepper, and Oregano. Roughly 8 minutes - set chicken aside.
  2. In the same skillet add butter and bloom the garlic. Add cherry tomatoes and season with salt and pepper. Cook tomatoes until they begin to burst then add spinach.
  3. Once the spinach begins to wilt from the heat add the heavy cream and parmesan. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer until the sauce has been reduced/thickened. Add chicken back to the pan and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
